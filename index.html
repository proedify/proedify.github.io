Our Mission

ProEdify’s mission is simple—give pre-professional students the community and support they need to succeed on their career path. ProEdify aims to be the comprehensive resource and community for students who not only want to obtain a certification, but master the fundamentals of their careers.

What We Offer

ProEdify is an educational channel dedicated to improving the lives of nursing and university students. Our mission is to reduce the stress that comes with studying and learning difficult material. We keep our videos short and simple for on-the-go review. Currently, our team members are developing a TEAS test prep course. And in the future, we plan to develop course content for Anatomy and Physiology, Nursing Fundamentals, Medical Surgical Nursing and more.
